================================================================================
  PART A: REGULAR MEETING KA POORA FLOW
================================================================================

Neeche step-by-step poora journey bataya gaya hai ek regular meeting
ka — shuru se end tak.


STEP 1: MEETING SCHEDULE KARNA
-------------------------------
  - Admin portal kholega aur "Schedule Meeting" pe click karega.
  - Ek form khulega jismein bharein:
      > Meeting ka naam (jaise "Weekly Review")
      > Company ka naam
      > Meeting kis type ki hai (Regular, Committee, wagairah)
      > Online hai ya Offline
      > Jagah ya Google Meet/Zoom ka link
      > Date aur Time
      > Kaun host kar raha hai
  - Phir agenda add karein — meeting mein kya kya discuss hoga.
  - Phir attendees (logon ko) add karein — unka naam, email,
    designation, WhatsApp number, aur koi special remark.
  - Jab sab bhar dein, toh neeche [Schedule & Send Invites] dabayein.

  >> SYSTEM KYA KAREGA:
     - Meeting save ho jayegi.
     - Jinke email dale hain, unko turant ek sundar meeting
       invitation email chala jayega jismein meeting ki saari
       details hongi.


STEP 2: MEETING HOGI — AUR ADMIN RECORDING SHURU KAREGA
---------------------------------------------------------
  - Scheduled date aur time pe meeting hogi (online ya offline).
  - Admin portal kholega aur us scheduled meeting ki "Details Page"
    pe jayega.
  - Details page pe upar ek "Recording Panel" dikhega jismein:
      > Ek microphone ka dropdown hoga — usme select karein ki
        kaunsa microphone/speaker use karna hai (jaise laptop ka
        built-in mic, ya koi external mic).
      > Neeche ek bada button hoga: [Start Intelligence Capture]

  >> KAB RECORDING SHURU KAREIN?
     - Jab meeting shuru ho jaaye aur discussion ka pehla shabd
       bola jaaye, TAB [Start Intelligence Capture] button dabayein.
     - Button dabte hi:
         > Screen ke neeche ek floating panel aa jayega jismein:
             - Timer chalega (00:00:00 se aage badhta hua)
             - Ek green signal dikhega jab awaaz aa rahi hogi
               ("Voice Signal Detected")
             - Agar khamoshi ho, toh likhega "No Sound Detected..."
             - Ek Pause button hoga — agar beech mein break lena
               ho toh dabayein, phir Play dabayein continue karne ke liye
         > Ye panel tab tak dikhega jab tak recording chal rahi hai.

  >> KAB RECORDING BAND KAREIN?
     - Jab meeting ki discussion poori ho jaaye — jaise sab log
       bye bol dein ya agenda khatam ho jaaye — tab floating panel
       mein laal [Finish] button dabayein.
     - Finish dabte hi recording ruk jayegi aur system turant AI
       ko bhej dega processing ke liye.

  ZAROORI TIPS:
    - Recording ke beech mein browser band mat karein warna
      recording kho jayegi.
    - Microphone computer ke paas rakhein taaki saari awaazein
      saaf record hon.
    - Agar meeting online hai (Google Meet pe), toh laptop ka
      speaker ON rakhein aur mic ke zariye speaker ki awaaz record
      ho jayegi.


STEP 3: AI KAAM SHURU KARTA HAI (AUTOPILOT MODE)
--------------------------------------------------
  [Finish] dabte hi poora screen ek "Analyzing Meeting Data"
  message dikhata hai — iska matlab AI ab kaam kar raha hai.

  AI ye saara kaam KHUD karta hai, bina kisi ke haath lagaye:

  1) SUNNA (Awaaz Se Likhawat):
     AI pehle poori recording sunta hai — chahe Hindi mein ho,
     English mein ho, ya dono milake (Hinglish). Har baat karne
     wale ko alag pehchanta hai — jaise "Speaker A ne ye bola,
     Speaker B ne ye bola". Poori baat likh li jaati hai
     shabd-dar-shabd.

  2) SAMJHNA (Summary Banana):
     Phir AI us poori likhi hui baat ko padhta hai aur samjhta
     hai ki:
       - Kya kya important baatein hui?
       - Kya decisions liye gaye?
       - Kya kya kaam karne hain?
     In sab se ek saaf-suthri, professional summary banata hai
     English mein.

  3) DOCUMENTS BANANA (3 PDF Files):
     AI teen documents banata hai:
       - Document 1: Poora transcript — har bola gaya shabd jaise
         ka taisa likha hua.
       - Document 2: AI ka apna log — usne kaise analyze kiya,
         step by step.
       - Document 3: Executive briefing — ek polished, readable
         summary.

  4) GOOGLE DRIVE MEIN SAVE KARNA:
     Teenon documents plus original audio recording automatic
     Google Drive mein upload ho jaate hain. Har meeting ka apna
     alag folder banta hai wahan.

  5) DATABASE UPDATE:
     System mein meeting ki entry update ho jaati hai — sab links
     jud jaate hain (recording ka link, summary ka link, transcript
     ka link).

  Ye poora process 2-5 minute mein hota hai (recording ki length
  ke hisaab se). Jab tak AI kaam kar raha hai, meeting ka status
  "Processing" dikhta hai.


STEP 4: ADMIN MOM FINALIZE KARTA HAI (Attendance + Tasks)
-----------------------------------------------------------
  AI ka kaam hone ke baad, Admin phir se us meeting pe jaata hai
  aur [Record MOM] pe click karta hai. Ab ek form khulega:

  a) ATTENDANCE MARK KARNA:
     - Har attendee ke aage dropdown hai — "Present", "Absent",
       ya "Excused" mein se ek chunein.
     - Zaroorat ho toh personal remark likh dein.

  b) DISCUSSION SUMMARY:
     - Ye box AI ne KHUD BHAR DIYA HOGA pehle se! Admin sirf
       padh ke dekhein aur agar kuch badalna ho toh edit kar lein.

  c) TASKS / ACTION ITEMS ADD KARNA:
     - [+ Add Task] pe click karke batayein ki meeting mein
       kya kya kaam decide hua:
         > Kya kaam hai (Title)
         > Kaun karega (attendees ki list se ya manually naam likhein)
         > Uska email
         > Kab tak karna hai (Deadline)
     - Ek se zyada tasks add kar sakte hain.

  d) NEXT MEETING (Optional):
     - Agar agle meeting ki date/time tay hui hai, toh woh
       bhi bhar dein.

  - Sab bharne ke baad, neeche [Save & Finalize MOM] dabayein.


STEP 5: FINAL EMAILS AUTOMATIC JAATE HAIN
-------------------------------------------
  Jab MOM save hota hai, system khud ye emails bhejta hai:

  EMAIL 1 — MOM SUMMARY (Sabhi Attendees Ko):
    - Har attendee jiska email diya tha, usko ek professional
      email jaata hai jismein:
        > Email ke andar meeting ki poori summary likhi hoti hai.
        > Email ke saath ek PDF file attached hoti hai — official
          MOM document.
        > Saare tasks ki list ek table mein hoti hai.

  EMAIL 2 — TASK ASSIGNMENT (Sirf Jinko Task Mila):
    - Agar kisi aadmi ko koi task assign hua hai, toh usko ek
      ALAG email jaata hai jismein:
        > Kaunsa task unko diya gaya hai.
        > Kis meeting se aaya hai.
        > Kab tak karna hai (deadline).

  EMAIL 3 — DEADLINE REMINDERS (Automatic, Roz):
    - System roz check karta hai ki kisi ka deadline aane wala
      hai ya nahi. Agar hai, toh yaad-dihaani email bhejta hai.

  EMAIL 4 — OVERDUE ALERTS (Automatic, Roz):
    - Agar kisi ka deadline nikal gaya aur task abhi tak complete
      nahi hua, toh ek warning email jaata hai.

  >> REGULAR MEETING KA FLOW KHATAM! <<


================================================================================
  PART B: BOARD RESOLUTION (BR) KA POORA FLOW
================================================================================

Board Resolution ka flow bilkul Regular Meeting jaisa hi hai,
bas kuch extra cheezein hain:


STEP 1: BR SCHEDULE KARNA
---------------------------
  - Schedule Meeting form mein "Meeting Type" dropdown mein
    "Board Resolution" select karein.
  - Baaki sab waisa hi — title, date, time, attendees (directors),
    wagairah.
  - [Schedule & Send Invites] dabayein.

  >> SYSTEM KYA KAREGA:
     - BR save hogi "Board Resolutions" section mein (alag se).
     - Sabhi directors ko formal invitation email jayega — iska
       design zyada corporate aur governance style ka hota hai
       (gehra rang, formal bhaasha).


STEP 2: BR MEETING HOGI — RECORDING WAISA HI
-----------------------------------------------
  - Admin "Board Resolutions" section mein jaake us BR ki
    Details Page kholega.
  - Details page pe wahi Recording Panel dikhega — microphone
    chunein aur [Start Intelligence Capture] dabayein.
  - Meeting discussion chal rahi ho tab tak recording chalegi.
  - Khatam hone pe [Finish] dabayein.
  - Bilkul waisa hi kaam karta hai jaise Regular Meeting mein.


STEP 3: AI WAISA HI KAAM KAREGA
---------------------------------
  - Audio sunna → Text likhna → Summary banana → 3 PDFs banana →
    Google Drive mein upload → Database update.
  - Sab bilkul waise hi hoga, fark sirf ye hai ki emails ka
    design zyada formal hota hai BR meetings ke liye.


STEP 4: ADMIN BR KA MOM FINALIZE KAREGA
-----------------------------------------
  - "Board Resolutions" mein jaake us BR pe [Record MOM] dabayein.
  - Form almost waisa hi hai, bas:
      > Attendees ke saath "Director" jaisi designation dikhti hai.
      > Tasks ko "Action Mandates" kaha jaata hai.
      > Supporting documents bhi upload kar sakte hain (jaise
        signed resolution ki scan copy).
  - Attendance mark karein, tasks add karein.
  - [Save & Finalize Resolution] dabayein.


STEP 5: FINAL EMAILS (Regular Wale + EXTRA CS WALA)
-----------------------------------------------------
  Sabhi attendees (directors) ko MOM email jaata hai (waisa hi
  jaise regular meeting mein — summary + PDF attached + tasks list).

  PAR — BR mein ek EXTRA step hai:

  ╔════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
  ║         COMPANY SECRETARY (CS) KO BHEJNA                 ║
  ║         (Sirf Board Resolutions Mein)                    ║
  ╚════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

  - BR Details page pe ek special button hota hai:
    [Send to CS]

  - Jab Admin ye button dabayega, toh ek popup aayega:
    "Enter CS Email (Leave blank to use default)"

  - Admin ya toh:
      > CS ka email daal sakta hai, YA
      > Khaali chhod de — system default CS email pe bhejega
        (jo pehle se set hai system mein).

  - OK dabayein.

  >> SYSTEM KYA KAREGA:
     - System us BR meeting ka ek official PDF banayega.
     - Woh PDF CS ke email pe bhej dega ek formal email ke saath.
     - System mein mark ho jayega ki "CS ko bhej diya gaya hai".

  Ye sirf Board Resolutions mein hota hai, regular meetings mein nahi.
  Iska matlab hai ki formal governance ka compliance maintain ho
  raha hai — Company Secretary ke paas company ke saare Board
  decisions ki official copy pahunch jaati hai.
